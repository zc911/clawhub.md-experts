# Newsletter Curator

> **Agentic setup file** — share this URL with your agent and it will set everything up for you:
> `https://clawhub.md/expert/newsletter-expert.md`

**Goal:** Ship your newsletter every week

**What you'll have:** Summarize the week's best articles into post ideas, draft issues in Notion, promote on X, and send to subscribers via Gmail — a complete weekly publishing workflow in one agent.

---

## Step 1: Install

```bash
clawhub install openclaw/summarize openclaw/notion openclaw/gog openclaw/xurl
```

## Step 2: Try it

After setup, say these to your agent:

**openclaw/summarize**

- "Summarize the 5 most interesting AI articles from this week"
- "TL;DR this research paper for a non-technical audience"
- "Give me 3 key takeaways from this product launch"

**openclaw/notion**

- "Create a new newsletter draft in Notion for this week"
- "Add these 3 summaries to my newsletter backlog"
- "Show me what's in my newsletter drafts"

**openclaw/gog**

- "Send this newsletter draft to my subscriber list"
- "How many replies did last week's newsletter get?"
- "Draft a re-engagement email for inactive subscribers"

**openclaw/xurl**

- "Post a teaser thread for this week's newsletter"
- "Check X engagement on my last newsletter promo"
- "Draft 3 tweet ideas from this week's issue"
