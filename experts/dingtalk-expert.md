# DingTalk Meeting Assistant

> **Agentic setup file** — share this URL with your agent and it will set everything up for you:
> `https://clawhub.md/expert/dingtalk-expert.md`

**Goal:** Automate DingTalk meetings end-to-end

**What you'll have:** Pull meeting agenda and materials before the call, then auto-generate minutes and send them to all attendees.

---

## Step 1: Install

```bash
clawhub install openclaw/dingtalk
```

## Step 2: Try it

After setup, say these to your agent to verify everything works:

**openclaw/dingtalk**

- "Prepare background materials for my 3pm meeting"
- "Draft meeting minutes from what we just discussed"
- "Send the minutes to all attendees via DingTalk"
